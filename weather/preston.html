Preston Idaho Triathlon

This Coming Saturday at 10:00 AM Preston will be hosting their 5th annual triathlon! Everyone is welcome for registration and teams are allowed! A triathlon is a multisport event that involves swimming, cycling, and running in that order. This triathlon will include swimming 0.5 miles, cycling 12 miles, and running 3 miles. There is a 20 dollar entry fee per individual. All profits will go to towards our local food kitchen. For more information contact Shelly Jones at shellyjones11@gmail.com.
